How to make a Kimchi Pancake

By. Lewis Rho

Time: 10 minutes

Jeon(ì „) is Korea's version of a pancake, similar to French Crepes, etc.

This recipe requires:

    • 35g of All Purpose Flour
      45g Potato Starch
      1 Cup of Kimchi
      1/4 Cup of Kimchi Juice
      1 Egg
      2 Tablespoons of Water
      2 Scallions
      100g Pork Belly
      4 Tablespoons of Vegetable Oil

    • Steps:

    • Take the flour and starch and mix them together
      Take the mixture and mix in the kimchi, egg, and water
      Add the kimchi juice, pork belly, and scallions together
      Mix both together but do not overmix
      Heat the frying pan over medium, and add in the oil
      Pour the batter into the pan, not allowing it to get too thick
      Wait until one side is brown, then flip
      Wait until both sides are browned and that the edges are cooked, then take out and serve
